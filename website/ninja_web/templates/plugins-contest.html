Plugins Contest !

The aim of this contest is to encourage the users of NINJA-IDE to write some plugins to help us improving the support of NINJA-IDE and get a prize as a recognition of their contribution.
You can submit your own plugins and vote for them or any other in this website starting at October 22nd 23rd 24th.

Important Dates

Start Date: October 22nd. Using extra ninja powers to start as soon as posible.
End Date: December 22nd. (This day we will announce the winners)

How to participate

  1. Sign up in NINJA-IDE's site.
  2. Submit your plugin (you can submit as many as you want, but only the one with the highest score will participate in the contest).

Checkout aut the wiki "Learn how to write a plugin".

Prizes

The first, second and third plugin with the highest scores will receive the following prizes. Ca-ching!!

  1. u$s 250
  2. u$s 150
  3. u$s 100

The delivery of the prize will be arranged with each winner.
Every user can win just one prize.
NINJA-IDE commiters are not able to participate in this contest.

How the winner will be chosen

The three first plugins with the higher score will be announced as the winners.
In case of a tie in any of the categories, the period to vote will be extended 2 more weeks just for the plugins under that situation.
If another tie is produced after the additional 2 weeks, the NINJA-IDE commiters will emit a vote for each plugins in order to resolve this situation.

How the scores are determine

Each plugin can receive a score from 1 to 5 for any user registered in the site.
The score of each plugin could be checked at any time through this website, and the score will be the average of the votes received until that moment.
